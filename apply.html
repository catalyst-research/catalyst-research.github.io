Applications

Interested in being an author or an editor?

Do you have a passion for research and want to share your findings with the world? Join our team as an author or editor! Authors get the opportunity to publish their interdisciplinary work, while editors help shape the voice of The Catalyst by reviewing submissions, guiding writers, and ensuring our publication maintains the highest academic standards. No prior experience is required—just dedication and a love for learning.

Interested in being a board member?

Looking to take on a leadership role and help drive the future of undergraduate research? Board members at The Catalyst are responsible for organizing events, managing our digital platform, expanding our outreach, and supporting our editorial staff. It is a fantastic way to build your resume, network with driven peers and faculty, and make a lasting impact on the UC San Diego research community.

The Catalyst is a student run digital paper series based at UC San Diego empowering undergraduate research voices.
