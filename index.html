About Us

The Catalyst is a student run digital paper series based at UC San Diego which aims to give voices to undergraduate students who have a passion for research. We welcome any and all students who are interested in publishing their work, and we are dedicated to fostering a diverse community of interdisciplinary scholarship.

We emphasize helping new students find their path in research, and aim to help publish new (and especially underrepresented) scholars. This includes expanding to include students from other institutions, with a focus on public health research papers.

    Our Mission:

    Give voices to undergraduate students passionate about research, with a focus on public health research papers.

    No discrimination based on race, color, national origin, religion, sex, gender, disability, age, medical condition, ancestry, marital status, citizenship, or sexual orientation.

    We are an academic organization connected to UC San Diego Undergraduate Research Hub.

Our Goal

This journal was founded with the intention of supporting those who are passionate about their studies. We encourage any student interested in research to get involved. Seriously.

We understand that innovation comes in many different forms. Whether a scholar is distinguished in their field or is brand new to research, we are here to help them grow. Whether a scholar is publishing a paper on public health or other interdisciplinary work, we are here to feature and celebrate their research.

For more info, please visit our UC San Diego eScholarship Site.
